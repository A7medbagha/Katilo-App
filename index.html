App Features

Weekly Orders

Branches place weekly product orders directly with the main factory.

Product Details

View comprehensive details and specifications for all dairy products.

Branch Tracking

View branch locations on integrated maps for logistics coordination.

PDF Receipts

Download PDF receipts for submitted orders and deliveries.

Product Browsing

Browse the entire catalog of dairy products with images and details.

Order Placement

Seamlessly place orders specifying quantities and delivery preferences.

Cash/Visa Pay

Flexible payment options including cash on delivery and Visa.

Item Returns

Initiate returns for purchased items directly through the app.

Chatbot Support

Instant customer support through an intelligent chatbot.
